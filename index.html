My name is Christopher Cabezas, and I am a postdoc researcher at the Université de Liège, Liège, Belgium.

My research interests are mainly centered in Multidimensional symbolic dynamics, Topological dynamics and Ergodic theory for Z^{d}-actions and its interactions with different fields of mathamtics, such as automata theory, combinatorics on words, finite geometry, fractal geometry, and number theory.
