I am a currently postdoc researcher at the Department of Mathematics at Université de Liège, Liège, Belgium.

In September 2022, I received my Ph.D. at LAMFA from Université de Picardie Jules Verne under the supervision of Samuel Petite. In May 2019, I completed the Master 2 of Fundamental Mathematics at Sorbonne Université and in March 2018 I completed a Master's degree at Department of Mathematical Engineering (Universidad de Chile) under the direction of Alejandro Maass. Since January 2023, I organize the Discrete Mathematics's seminar at Université de Liège.

My research interests are mainly centered in Multidimensional symbolic dynamics (specifically minimal Cantor systems), Topological dynamics and Ergodic theory for Z^{d}-actions and its interactions with different fields of mathamtics, such as automata theory, combinatorics on words, finite geometry, fractal geometry, and number theory.
